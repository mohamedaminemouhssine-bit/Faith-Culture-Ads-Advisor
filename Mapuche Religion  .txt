Mapuche Religion (Chile, Argentina)

Key Beliefs:
- Spiritual connection to land and nature
- Worship of spirits and ancestral beings
- Use of machi (shamans) for rituals

Cultural Values:
- Respect for elders and ancestors
- Community orientation
- Nature conservation

Marketing Tips:
- Highlight nature-friendly and community-oriented messages
- Avoid sacred imagery misuse
- Use culturally appropriate symbols and colors

Taboos:
- Do not misrepresent machi or rituals
- Avoid sacred sites in advertising images
- Avoid trivializing spiritual practices
