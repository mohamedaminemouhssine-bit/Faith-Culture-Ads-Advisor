Akan / Ashanti Religion (Ghana)

Key Beliefs:
- Belief in Supreme Being and ancestral spirits
- Use of Adinkra symbols for values and messages
- Festivals and ceremonies are central

Cultural Values:
- Community harmony
- Respect for tradition
- Symbolism in colors and patterns

Marketing Tips:
- Use culturally respected symbols (Adinkra) carefully
- Align campaigns with festival seasons
- Highlight family and community values

Taboos:
- Do not misrepresent sacred symbols
- Avoid disrespecting chiefs or elders
- Avoid religiously sensitive colors in the wrong context
