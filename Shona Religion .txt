Shona Religion (Zimbabwe)

Key Beliefs:
- Worship of Mwari (supreme being) through spirits
- Ancestor reverence
- Divination and spirit mediums

Cultural Values:
- Respect for elders
- Rituals for life transitions
- Strong connection to land and nature

Marketing Tips:
- Avoid images or claims that offend ancestors
- Incorporate nature-related symbols subtly
- Respect local ceremonies and festivals

Taboos:
- Avoid direct criticism of spiritual leaders
- Do not use sacred objects in advertising
- Avoid insensitive language about death or spirits
