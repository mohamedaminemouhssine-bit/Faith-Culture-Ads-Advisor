Maasai Religion (Kenya, Tanzania)

Key Beliefs:
- Ancestor worship
- Belief in Enkai (Supreme God)
- Rituals around cattle and nature

Cultural Values:
- Strong community ties
- Respect for elders
- Ritual purity and age-set systems

Marketing Tips:
- Use culturally appropriate colors (red, blue, white)
- Avoid imagery disrespecting cattle
- Highlight community and family connections

Taboos:
- Avoid portraying sacred rituals inaccurately
- Do not show sacred ceremonies casually
- Avoid offensive images or language about cattleg context
