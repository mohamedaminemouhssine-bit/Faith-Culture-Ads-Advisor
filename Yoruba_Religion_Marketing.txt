Yoruba Religion (Nigeria, Benin, Togo)

Key Beliefs:
- Worship of Orishas (deities representing natural forces)
- Ancestor veneration
- Respect for spiritual harmony

Cultural Values:
- Community orientation
- Symbolism in colors and objects
- Rituals for important life events

Marketing Tips:
- Use colors with spiritual significance (e.g., white for purity, blue for wisdom)
- Respect festivals and sacred days
- Avoid depictions of sacred symbols in trivial contexts

Taboos:
- Do not disrespect Orishas or ancestors
- Avoid misleading claims about spiritual powers
- Avoid offensive imagery during religious ceremonies
