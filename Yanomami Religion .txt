Yanomami Religion (Amazon, Brazil/Venezuela)

Key Beliefs:
- Animism: all living things have spirits
- Shamanic rituals for healing and protection
- Connection to forest spirits (xapiripë)

Cultural Values:
- Community cohesion
- Respect for nature and spirits
- Oral traditions

Marketing Tips:
- Respect nature-related spiritual beliefs
- Avoid using sacred images or rituals in ads
- Highlight communal and sustainable values

Taboos:
- Do not depict shamans inaccurately
- Avoid disrespecting sacred plants and animals
- Avoid offensive or exploitative imagery
