Guarani Religion (Paraguay, Brazil, Argentina)

Key Beliefs:
- Animism: spirits in nature
- Ancestral veneration
- Rituals for protection and harmony

Cultural Values:
- Community and family orientation
- Respect for elders and nature
- Oral storytelling traditions

Marketing Tips:
- Use eco-friendly and community-focused messages
- Avoid sacred symbols in trivial contexts
- Show respect for ancestral and nature traditions

Taboos:
- Avoid using sacred ceremonies in ads
- Do not disrespect spiritual leaders
- Avoid offensive imagery related to nature or ancestors
