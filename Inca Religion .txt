Inca Religion (Peru, Andes)

Key Beliefs:
- Worship of Inti (Sun God) and Pachamama (Earth Mother)
- Ancestor veneration
- Ceremonial festivals (Inti Raymi)

Cultural Values:
- Harmony with nature
- Community and reciprocity
- Symbolism in textiles and colors

Marketing Tips:
- Respect nature and spiritual symbols
- Use traditional patterns and motifs carefully
- Align campaigns with cultural festivals when possible

Taboos:
- Avoid misrepresenting sacred festivals
- Do not disrespect Pachamama or Inti
- Avoid offensive depictions of ancestors
